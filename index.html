Pureskill.gg

The world’s first automated coach for CS:GO. Using machine learning, AI, and statistics to show you what you’re doing wrong and how to fix it.

We are currently in open alpha and you can currently use our 2D demo viewer to analyze your own games

WHAT IS IT?

Pureskill.gg is a service that will take a CS:GO player’s demo, pull it apart, analyze what happened, and provide tailored feedback to help them understand what they are doing wrong and how to fix it.
